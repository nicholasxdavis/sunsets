New Mexico Cannabis Laws

The following information is provided for educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Always consult with a qualified attorney for legal matters.

Recreational Cannabis Legalization

New Mexico legalized recreational cannabis for adults 21 and older on April 1, 2022, under the Cannabis Regulation Act.

Key Legal Provisions

  • Adults 21+ can possess up to 2 ounces of cannabis flower
  • Adults 21+ can possess up to 16 grams of cannabis extract
  • Adults 21+ can grow up to 6 plants per person (12 per household)
  • Cannabis must be consumed in private residences
  • Driving under the influence of cannabis is illegal

Las Cruces Specific Regulations

Las Cruces follows New Mexico state cannabis laws. Local ordinances may apply, so check with city officials for specific regulations in your area.

Federal Considerations

Cannabis remains illegal under federal law. This creates potential conflicts between state and federal law, particularly regarding employment, housing, and federal benefits.
